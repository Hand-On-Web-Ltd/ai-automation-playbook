# Chapter 4: Costs and ROI

Let's talk money. How much does AI automation actually cost, and when do you make it back?

## Typical Costs

### Chatbots (Website / WhatsApp)

| Type | Setup Cost | Monthly Cost |
|------|-----------|--------------|
| Basic FAQ bot (no-code) | £0-500 | £0-50 |
| Smart chatbot with AI | £1,000-3,000 | £50-200 |
| Full customer service bot | £3,000-8,000 | £100-400 |

### Voice AI Phone Agents

| Type | Setup Cost | Monthly Cost |
|------|-----------|--------------|
| Simple greeting + voicemail | £500-1,500 | £50-150 |
| Appointment booking agent | £2,000-5,000 | £100-300 |
| Full phone handling system | £5,000-15,000 | £200-600 |

### Workflow Automation

| Type | Setup Cost | Monthly Cost |
|------|-----------|--------------|
| Simple two-app connection | £0-200 | £0-30 |
| Multi-step workflow | £500-2,000 | £30-100 |
| Complex business process | £2,000-10,000 | £50-300 |

These are rough UK figures for small businesses. Your actual costs depend on complexity, volume, and who builds it.

## Calculating Your ROI

Here's the simple version:

**Time saved per month** × **Cost of that time** = **Monthly savings**

### Example: Dental Practice Phone Bot

- Receptionist handles 30 appointment calls/day
- Each call takes ~4 minutes
- That's 2 hours/day on appointment calls = 44 hours/month
- Receptionist costs £14/hour
- Monthly cost of those calls: **£616**
- Voice AI handles 80% of appointment calls
- Monthly savings: **£493**
- Setup cost: £3,000
- Monthly running cost: £150
- Net monthly saving: **£343**
- **Break even: ~9 months**

After that, you're saving £343 every month. Plus your receptionist is doing more useful work.

### Example: Lead Qualification Chatbot

- Sales team gets 200 enquiries/month
- Spends 15 minutes qualifying each one
- 50 hours/month on qualification
- Team member costs £20/hour
- Monthly cost: **£1,000**
- Chatbot qualifies 70% of leads automatically
- Monthly savings: **£700**
- Setup cost: £2,500
- Monthly running cost: £100
- Net monthly saving: **£600**
- **Break even: ~4 months**

## The Stuff That's Harder to Measure

Some benefits don't show up in a simple cost calculation:

- **24/7 availability** — never miss an after-hours enquiry again
- **Faster response times** — customers get answers in seconds, not hours
- **Consistency** — the AI gives the same quality response every time (no bad days)
- **Happier team** — nobody enjoys doing the same task 50 times a day
- **Scalability** — handle 10x the volume without hiring

## When It's NOT Worth It

Be honest about this:

- If a process only takes 30 minutes a week, the ROI probably isn't there
- If the task requires constant human judgment, automation won't help much
- If you're only going to use it for 3 months, the setup cost might not pay back
- If the volume is tiny (5 enquiries/week), keep doing it manually

## The Real Question

It's not "can we afford to automate?" — it's "can we afford not to?"
