# Chapter 2: Common Use Cases

Here's where AI automation actually works well for small businesses. These aren't theoretical — we've built all of these for real clients.

## 1. Customer Service Chatbots

**The problem:** You get the same 20 questions over and over. Your team spends hours answering them.

**The fix:** A chatbot on your website or WhatsApp that handles FAQs, collects enquiry details, and only passes the tricky stuff to a human.

**Works well for:** Any business that gets regular customer questions — trades, clinics, agencies, shops.

## 2. Voice AI Phone Agents

**The problem:** Missed calls = missed business. You can't always pick up the phone, especially after hours.

**The fix:** An AI agent that answers calls, has a natural conversation, books appointments, takes messages, and sends you a summary.

**Works well for:** Dental practices, hair salons, trades, estate agents — anyone who relies on phone bookings.

## 3. Lead Qualification

**The problem:** You get loads of enquiries but half of them aren't serious. Your sales team wastes time on tyre-kickers.

**The fix:** An AI that asks qualifying questions (budget, timeline, needs) before passing leads to your team. Bad leads get filtered out automatically.

**Works well for:** Agencies, B2B services, consultancies, high-ticket businesses.

## 4. Appointment Booking

**The problem:** Back-and-forth emails and phone calls just to find a time that works.

**The fix:** AI that checks your calendar, suggests available slots, and books appointments — via chat, phone, or email.

**Works well for:** Pretty much any service business.

## 5. Data Entry and Admin

**The problem:** Someone on your team spends hours copying info between spreadsheets, CRMs, and accounting software.

**The fix:** Automation that pulls data from one system and puts it in another. AI handles the messy bits — reading invoices, extracting info from emails, matching records.

**Works well for:** Accountants, property managers, e-commerce, anyone drowning in spreadsheets.

## 6. Email Triage and Response

**The problem:** Your inbox is chaos. Important emails get buried under junk and newsletters.

**The fix:** AI that reads incoming emails, categorises them, drafts responses for common queries, and flags the urgent ones.

**Works well for:** Anyone with a busy inbox (so... everyone).

## 7. Social Media and Content

**The problem:** Keeping up with social media posts takes more time than it should.

**The fix:** AI that helps draft posts, suggests content ideas, and schedules publishing. Not fully automatic — you still review and approve — but way faster than starting from scratch.

**Works well for:** Small businesses trying to stay active online without hiring a social media person.

## 8. Invoice Processing

**The problem:** Manually entering invoice details into your accounting software.

**The fix:** AI reads invoices (PDF, email, photo), extracts the key info (amount, date, supplier), and feeds it into your system.

**Works well for:** Any business that processes more than a handful of invoices per week.

## The Common Thread

Notice the pattern? Every use case here is about the same thing: **taking repetitive, predictable tasks and letting software handle them.** The AI bit just makes it smarter about dealing with the messy, real-world inputs that traditional automation can't handle.
